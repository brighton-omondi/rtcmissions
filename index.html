RTC Ministries

Make it stand out

At RTC Medical Centre, we recognize the importance of providing holistic care to our patients including spiritual and social needs that accompany physical ailments while we can treat the body, only God heals the mind, body and soul.

  • Our mission

    To care for and transform the lives of children and families in underprivileged communities through Christian outreach, medical care and sustainable community development.

  • Our Mission

    To unite people in alleviating suffering and despair through Christian discipleship, meeting basic healthcare needs, establishing self-reliance and promoting environmental stewardship even as we grow In God’s Kingdom.
